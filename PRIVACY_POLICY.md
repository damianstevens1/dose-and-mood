# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

GLP-1 Mood, also listed as GLP-1 Mental Health Tracker, is a personal tracking app for mood, anxiety, energy, appetite, sleep, GLP-1 medication notes, dose timing, and side effect notes.

This app is for personal tracking and organization only. It does not diagnose, treat, predict risk, recommend medication changes, or replace medical care.

## Information You Enter

The app lets you enter optional information such as:

- Name or nickname
- GLP-1 medication and dose notes
- Mood, anxiety, energy, appetite, sleep, nausea, GI, and side effect notes
- Dose day, start date, reminders, and related tracking notes

## How Information Is Stored

The app is designed to store tracking information locally on your device.

We do not require an account. We do not operate a server for storing your tracking entries. We do not sell your information.

If you delete the app, locally stored app data may be deleted from your device depending on your device settings and backups.

## Sharing and Exports

The app includes export and sharing tools. These tools create a summary or log that you may choose to share with a clinician, caregiver, or another person.

Sharing is controlled by you. When you use your device's share sheet, the selected destination may handle the information according to that service's privacy practices.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app may use local notifications on your device. Reminder settings are controlled by your device settings and the app's reminder controls.

## Third Parties

We do not use the app to sell personal data, show third-party advertising, or provide unrestricted web browsing.

Apple may process information related to app downloads, purchases, crash reports, diagnostics, and TestFlight according to Apple's own privacy policies and settings.

## Health and Safety

The app may include crisis support information or links for urgent help. If you may hurt yourself or need immediate support, contact local emergency services or a crisis line immediately.
