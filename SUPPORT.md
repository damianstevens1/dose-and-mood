# GLP-1 Mental Health Tracker Support

Thank you for using GLP-1 Mental Health Tracker.

This app is a private personal tracking tool for mood, anxiety, energy, appetite, sleep, dose timing, and side effect notes during GLP-1 use.

The app is for personal tracking and organization only. It does not diagnose conditions, prove cause, predict risk, recommend medication changes, or replace medical care.

## Common Support Topics

### Data and Privacy

Tracking entries are designed to stay on your device. The app does not require an account and does not operate a server for storing your entries.

Privacy Policy:

https://github.com/damianstevens1/dose-and-mood/blob/main/PRIVACY_POLICY.md

### Exporting Information

The app includes export tools for creating a visit summary or full log. Sharing is controlled by you through your device's share options.

### Reminders

Reminder permissions and delivery are controlled by iOS notification settings and the app's reminder controls.

### Urgent Support

If you may hurt yourself or need immediate help, contact local emergency services or a crisis line immediately. In the United States, call or text 988.
